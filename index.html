About Us

Welcome to The Academy Barrackpore

Established: 29/03/2016

The Academy Barrackpore is dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development for students from Pre-School to Class 12. We offer coaching for all subjects across ICSE, ISC, CBSE, and W.B Board, as well as special classes for Olympiad, Spell Bee, and a variety of hobby and vocational courses.
